Guntur Kaaram[a] (pronounced Spice of Guntur) (also known by the initialism GK,) is an upcoming Indian Telugu-language action drama film written and directed by Trivikram Srinivas, and produced by S. Radha Krishna through Haarika & Hassine Creations. It stars Mahesh Babu, Sreeleela, Meenakshi Chaudhary, Jagapathi Babu, Ramya Krishna, Jayaram, Prakash Raj, and Brahmanandam. The film was officially announced in May 2021 under the tentative title SSMB28, as it is Mahesh's 28th film in the leading role , and the official title was announced in May 2023. Principal photography commenced in September 2022, mostly shot in Hyderabad, and wrapped by late-December 2023. The film's score and soundtrack are composed by Thaman S, with cinematography handled by Manoj Paramahamsa and editing handled by Naveen Nooli Guntur Kaaram is scheduled to be released in theatres worldwide on 12 January 2024, coinciding with Sankranti.

Naa Saami Ranga Trailer Release Date: "Naa Saami Ranga" is an upcoming Telugu period action drama directed by Vijay Binni, featuring an original story by Malayalam director Joshiy. The film stars Nagarjuna, Allari Naresh, Raj Tarun, Ashika Ranganath, and Jisshu Sengupta in pivotal roles portraying the t2024/naa-saami-rangitular characters.The film's original soundtrack is crafted by M. M. Keeravani, featuring lyrics penned by Chandra Bose. Eagle is an upcoming Indian Telugu-language action thriller film written and directed by cinematographer Karthik Gattamneni, who also previously directed Surya vs Surya (2015). It is produced by T. G. Vishwa Prasad and Vivek Kuchibhotla, under People Media Factory. It stars Ravi Teja in the titular role along with Anupama Parameswaran, Navdeep, Kavya Thapar, Srinivas Avasarala and Madhoo.[1] The film was officially announced on Jun 12, 2023, along with a teaser.[2][3] The film's score and soundtrack are composed by Davzand, with cinematography and editing handled by Gattamneni himself. Eagle is scheduled to be released in theatres worldwide on 9 February 2024.[4]

Devara: Part 1 (transl. God) is an upcoming Indian Telugu-language action-drama film written and directed by Koratala Siva. Produced by Sudhakar Mikkilineni and Kosaraju Harikrishna under the banners Yuvasudha Arts and N. T. R. Arts, the film stars N. T. Rama Rao Jr. and Janhvi Kapoor in the lead roles with Saif Ali Khan as the main antagonist. Anirudh Ravichander is composing the soundtrack, while R. Rathnavelu and A. Sreekar Prasad are handling cinematography and editing respectively. Production designer Sabu Cyril has been brought in as the production designer. The first part of the film is scheduled for a worldwide release on 5 April 2024.

Pushpa 2: The Rule is an upcoming Indian Telugu-language action drama film[2] written and directed by Sukumar, produced by Mythri Movie Makers in association with Sukumar Writings, it stars Allu Arjun as the titular character alongside Fahadh Faasil and Rashmika Mandanna, reprising their roles. It is a sequel to the 2021 film Pushpa: The Rise and the second installment in the Pushpa film series.[3] Made on a budget of ₹350crore,[1] the film is one of the most expensive Indian films to date. It is scheduled to release on 15 August 2024.[4]
